About Me

Born in the Philippines, and raised in Australia, I come from the best of both worlds. Going through high school, I didn't know what I wanted to do in the future. I had an IPT class which I did quite well in, and that gave me an idea of a career path I wanted to pursue. Post graduation, I took Cert IV in Programming. This was my first real exposure to coding. As challenging as it was, it was enjoyable. I wasn't just forcing myself to pass subjects. Every problem comes with a feeling of satisfaction once you solve it. It made me use my mind in ways I've never used it before. But I didn't continue that path, I didn't have enough confidence, and I wasn't ready to commit.

I've had the luxury of experimenting and exploring a career path suitable for me. I've completed courses such as the Diploma of Counselling, and Cert IV in Accounting, and I've had my fair share of work experience. Fast forward to 2023, I decided to give a second chance to the world of programming. Graduating from Academy Xi boot camp, I've gained the skills and knowledge to build a successful website with a positive experience. Gaining confidence from the other paths I took, and with the time ticking, I am now ready to commit and start my journey. This is only the beginning, and I'm excited to continue learning and expanding my skill set.
